Debugging Hours

A well-rested body is good for coding. Unfortunately that's not the case, especially for an entrepreneur like me. I wanted to check if there is a direct relation between number of hours sleeping and coding efficiency. So I embarked on a small project to track my hours. And here is my result. I realized, the more I sleep, the less I spent hours debugging my code. Sleep is good!
